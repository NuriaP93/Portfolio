About Me

Hello! I'm Nuria and I'm from Argentina. Drawing has been my passion since I was a child. I've been using digital media to share my work since 2009, so I've been developing and improving my skills on the subject since then.

I'm also a Electronic Engineering Student and a Website Developer (though I'm still new to the task). This personal portfolio is one of my first projects as a programmer!

Some trivia: Im an introvert. I'm left-handed. Painting artworks has never been a passion to me personally. I'm too perfectionist sometimes, and I also have to deal with anxiety problems. Tough combination that one, right?. I also love animals! I have three beautiful dogs!
